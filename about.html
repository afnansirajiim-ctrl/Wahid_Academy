Our Story

Wahid Academy was founded with a singular mission: to make authentic Arabic language education and Islamic studies accessible to every Muslim and Arabic language enthusiast around the world.

Our name, "Wahid" (واحد), meaning "one" in Arabic, reflects our commitment to unity — uniting students with the language of the Quran, and uniting knowledge-seekers from all corners of the globe.
